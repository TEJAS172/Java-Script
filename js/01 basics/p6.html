OPERATORS

JavaScript operators are symbols that are used to perform operations on operands,

        1, Arithmetic operators => Addition =, substraction -, multiplication *,division /, modulus %, increment ++, decrement --
        2, Comparison operators => is equal to ==, Identical(equal and of same data type)===, not equal to !=, not identical !==,Greator than>,

        3, bitwise operator => Bitwise add &, bitwise OR |,Bitwise XOR ^,Bitwise NOT ~,Bitwise left shift<<,
        Bitwise right shift>>, Bitwise rightshift with zero >>>

        4, Logical operator => logical AND &&,Logical OR ||,Logical NOT !

        5, Assignment operators => Assign =,Add and Assign +=, Subtract and assign -=,Multiply and assign *=, Divide and assign /=, Modulus and assign %=
        
        6, Special or Ternary operators => condition ? "true":"false";
